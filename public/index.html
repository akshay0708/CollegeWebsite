ABOUT US

A student group promoting general welfare of students with a common purpose of developing essential skills such as public speaking,stress and time managment overcoming bad habits such as smoking,driking and Career Guidance.

Mission:

Learning to live a stress free life in a technical world.

Objectives:

  1. To train in improving memory,concentration,self-confidence and will power.
  2. To guide students in their career counselling and job opportunities.
  3. To develope essential skills such as public speaking,stress and time managment,etc. By organising different seminars and presentations.
  4. To save youth from self destructive habits and addictions that harm the body and mind.
  5. To organise outings for higher experience with nature and God.
